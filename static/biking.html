Do you want to move your cycling on to the next level? Been cycling down the road, but want to explore the bridleways and beautiful green lanes in Yorkshire or near you?

Our one day course will give you the skills to ride off on your own adventure. The course will allow you to develop your personal riding skills, trailside maintenance, navigation, knowledge of equipment and route planning.

Gareth holds the CTC Technical Mountain Bike Leader award and has been leading groups for the past five years in the UK.
