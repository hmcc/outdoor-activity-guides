Gareth has a wealth of experience of working with young people of different outlook and abilities. He is able to enthuse and motivate individuals in the outdoors, whether they are confident or petrified. He ensures the challenge suits each individual and develops young people through his fun yet professional style. Gareth has worked with multiple groups delivering bronze, silver and gold Duke of Edinburgh's Award expeditions. He has a wealth of knowledge of local routes and campsites for practice and assessment expeditions, delivering a successful silver cycling expedition as well as walking expeditions in many different areas.
