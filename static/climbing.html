Climbing courses

Indoor climbing courses can be delivered at any indoor climbing wall.

Beginners course: This will provide you with the knowledge, skills and techniques to safely boulder and climb top roped walls.

Intermediate course: This course will provide you with the knowledge, skills and techniques for lead climbing.

Coaching: This is aimed at climbers who have the practical skills for top rope or lead climbing but wish to focus on improving their climbing performance. The course will explore movement skills as well as the principles and methods of training.

Rock climbing

This is aimed at people who have climbed indoors and would like to transfer and develop their skills to climb safely in the outdoors. We offer courses in all disciplines from bouldering to traditional, sport and multi pitch climbing.

Lead climbing

This course will introduce you to traditional climbing equipment, allowing you to develop knowledge skills and techniques in the placement of protection and creation of anchors to enable safe climbing on traditional crags.

Sport climbing

For a climber who is able to lead climb indoors, this is an introduction to sport climbing on some of Yorkshire's iconic limestone crags. This will give you the experience you need to get the most out of your days on the rock.

Multi pitch climbing

If you wish to push your outdoor climbing experience to new heights then this is for you. Multi pitch routes are longer routes, involving two or more pitches. This course is appropriate for climbers who have experience of lead climbing, sport or traditional.

Bouldering

Coaching / guided days can be organized for all abilities at Brimham rocks, Armscliff, Caley and more. Please contact us for more information.

Guiding and scrambling

If you wish to be guided up a classic route you have always dreamed of climbing then give us a call. Whether single, multi pitch, difficult or extreme grade, we can help you achieve your goal..

Abseiling

Experience abseiling on a countryside crag or in the heart of the city. Whether this is for a group of friends or a charity fundraising event we can organise this adventure event safely for you.

Holidays

Planning something further afield? We offer organised climbing holidays to the sunny limestone crags in Europe with full instruction, guiding, accommodation and food.
