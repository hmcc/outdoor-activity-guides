Gareth Haldenby manages Outdoor Activity Guides and is the team's chief instructor. Gareth has over eighteen years of personal outdoor experience and has ascended many classic routes across the world.

Gareth has been working in the outdoors for over twelve years and has experience of working with a variety of individuals and groups, tailoring sessions to meet specific needs. Gareth along with his team of instructors are experienced, qualified and highly skilled in coaching, instructing and guiding groups in outdoor activities.

Qualifications:

  • Mountain Instructor Award (MIA)
  • Single Pitch Award (SPA)
  • Mountain Leader (ML)
  • Level 2 Technical mountain bike leader (CTC)
  • Certificate in Education (College tutor)
  • BTEC in Outdoor Education
  • First Aid
