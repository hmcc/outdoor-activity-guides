Welcome

Outdoor Activity Guides is an excellent way to discover adventure activities and develop skills within the outdoors.

Do you want some great stories to come back into the office with on Monday morning? Email us to discuss your adventure plans: outdooractivityguides@gmail.com

Climbing

We provide instruction, coaching, guiding and training, catering for beginners through to advanced climbers. Sessions are tailored to meet individual needs and aspirations and can be delivered at a venue to suit you.

Bouldering

Coaching / guided days can be organized for all abilities at Brimham rocks, Armscliff, Caley and more.

Hill walking

Have the skills to set out on your own to explore some of the most beautiful wilderness in the British Isles.

We offer courses to meet individual aspirations, from learning to navigate whilst walking on main tracks through to mountainous terrain with poor visibility.

Mountain biking

Do you want to move your cycling on to the next level? We can help you to develop your personal riding skills, trailside maintenance, navigation, knowledge of equipment and route planning.

Technical advice

We can act as an technical advisors in line with licensing regulations for indoor climbing walls and offer site specific training and certification to centre staff and school teachers to ensure delivery is safe and in line with industry standards.

Holidays

Planning something further afield? We offer organised climbing holidays to the sunny limestone crags in Europe with full instruction, guiding, accommodation and food.
