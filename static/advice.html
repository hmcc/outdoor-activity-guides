Technical advisor for adventure activities

We offer consultancy, advice and staff training in mountaineering , rock climbing and other related activities.

Gareth can act as an technical advisor in line with licensing regulations for indoor climbing walls and offers site specific training and certification to centre staff and school teachers to ensure delivery is safe and in line with industry standards.

Climbing route setting service

Gareth has ten years experience in route setting on man made climbing walls. Having climbed all over the world for the past eighteen years, Gareth is able to draw on his experience to create varied routes and problems which challenge the climber to incorporate different styles.
