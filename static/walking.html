Navigation training

Want to be able to enjoy the countryside but not confident in your ability to navigate? We offer courses to meet individual aspirations, from learning to navigate whilst walking on main tracks through to mountainous terrain with poor visibility.

"I was never really sure how to use a map and compass so never really ventured far into the countryside unless with an organised party. Gareth supported me in developing my navigation skills which has opened up new experiences for me" Sandy

Expedition training

Have the skills to set out on your own to explore some of the most beautiful wilderness in the British Isles. Gain knowledge on equipment and develop camp craft skills essential for any expedition.

Supervising groups

Whatever the challenge we can support with the planning, organisation and leadership of expeditions in the UK, to ensure your group safely and effectively achieve their goal.

Gorge scrambling

Want to try an exciting new activity ? Abseil in, scramble up rocks, swim, slide and jump in plunge pools.

This is a thrilling adventure that will leave your feeling challenged and alive.
